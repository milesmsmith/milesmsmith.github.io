Quarky

This was a project that Miles embarked on with a group of friends to develop a 3D version of chess transposed onto the surface of a 5x5 Rubik's cube to create a challenging variant of chess that develops spatial reasoning and problem solving skills.

Rube Goldberg System

For a mechanical engineering design course, Miles developed an intricate system of simple machines with a team of undergraduates that connected with over ten other groups to set up a put on the green for an easy tap in! He developed foundational skills in simple machines for engineering design.

UROS

With a team, Miles developed a robot that overcame a challenging obstacle course and completed a set of missions to score points in a team competition. For this challenge, a robot was developed using a collection of Vex robotics parts, servos, and motors programmed using RobotC.

Elevator Design Challenge

The goal of design challenge was to develop a high efficiency elevator that could lift a 5-pound weight up 1-meter above the ground, while minimizing the cost and weight of the machine. By selecting a high efficiency gear ratio and a proper motor the weight was able to be lifted in under 1s!

Ramp Design Challenge

The purpose of this project was to develop a new ramp for handicapped member of the local community that needed a new ramp to be implemented in the yard of their yard that would allow them to safely entire the home. The design of the ramp was developed in accordance with the Wheelchair Ramp Requirements outlined by the Americans with Disabilities Act (ADA).
