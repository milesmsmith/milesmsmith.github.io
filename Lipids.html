Lipid Optimization for Biofuels

Through Dr. Joel Ducoste and the NC State Civil, Construction, and Environmental Engineering department I was able to assist in research that was seeking to improve the accuracy of a predictive mathematical model (using MATLAB) that sought to predict the accumulation of lipids for a specific type of marine microalgae.

The purpose of studying the lipid accumulation was to understand the opperation conditions and learn how to maximize lipid production for biofuel development. My findings on this study included finding a relationship between light intensity to capture lipid degradation that had been observed, which helped to significantly improve the prior mathematical model.
