Research Area

I am currently in the process of shifting topics into the domain of biomechanics. I am hoping to combine my backgrounds in running and engineering together to better understand human motion and design solutions to address challenges in biomechanics and keep people active. Previously, my work focused on understanding bulk and interfacial behavior of electrolytes to guide the design of next-generation batteries.
