Introduction

I am a first-year graduate student in the Electrochemical Energy Lab at MIT advised by Yang Shao-Horn working on developing new polymer electrolytes materials for applications in batteries. My current work focuses on electrolyte characterization using Raman spectroscopy, Fourier-transformed infrared (FTIR) spectroscopy, and electrochemical impedance spectroscopy and developing techniques to extract information from various spectra.
